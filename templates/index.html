About
Welcome to our innovative project focused on developing an affordable tobacco detector using the ESP-32 Wi-Fi module and MQ-135 gas sensor. Our goal is to address the health risks associated with tobacco consumption in restricted areas by creating a low-cost, easily deployable solution. The evolution of our problem statement emphasizes the core objectives of efficient power consumption, affordability, real-time detection, prompt alert mechanisms, and promoting public health awareness.

Our methodology involves carefully selecting the MQ-135 gas sensor for its sensitivity to tobacco-related gases, calibrating the sensor for accuracy, and assembling the hardware components, including the ESP-32 and power supply. Strategic sensor placement indoors and setting precise gas concentration thresholds ensure effective tobacco detection. We've implemented an alert mechanism using the Twilio web service API to notify relevant authorities when thresholds are exceeded.

Thorough testing and optimization are conducted to guarantee reliable performance before deploying the system in target areas. Ongoing monitoring and maintenance ensure the long-term operation of our tobacco detector, contributing to the creation of healthier, smoke-free environments. Join us in our mission to make tobacco detection accessible and promote smoking cessation for improved public health outcomes.
